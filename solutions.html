Practical Solutions

Discover actionable steps you can take to reduce your environmental impact in every area of your life.

Reduce Your Footprint

Energy

  • Switch to renewable energy providers
  • Install LED lighting and smart thermostats
  • Improve home insulation
  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Unplug devices when not in use

Transportation

  • Walk, bike, or use public transit
  • Consider electric or hybrid vehicles
  • Combine errands to reduce trips
  • Maintain proper tire pressure
  • Fly less and offset flight emissions

Food

  • Reduce meat and dairy consumption
  • Buy local and seasonal produce
  • Reduce food waste
  • Grow some of your own food
  • Choose sustainable seafood

Shopping

  • Buy less and choose quality items
  • Support sustainable brands
  • Bring reusable bags and containers
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Repair instead of replace

Waste

  • Recycle properly
  • Compost food scraps
  • Use reusable water bottles and coffee cups
  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Donate or sell unwanted items

Community

  • Educate others about sustainability
  • Support environmental policies
  • Join local conservation efforts
  • Participate in community gardens
  • Organize neighborhood cleanups

Carbon Offsetting

For emissions you can't eliminate, carbon offsets allow you to compensate by funding projects that reduce greenhouse gases elsewhere. Look for verified offset programs that meet high standards like Gold Standard or Verified Carbon Standard.
